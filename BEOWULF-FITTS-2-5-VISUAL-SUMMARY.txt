================================================================================
KEY COMPOSITIONAL INSIGHTS
================================================================================

1. HARMONIC CONTINUITY ACROSS FITTS
   Fitt 2's dissonance should echo through Fitts 3–5 as unspoken shadow.
   Grendel remains the underlying threat.

2. THE NADIR IS FITT 3, SCENE 2
   Heathen sacrifice is where human resources are exhausted.
   This should reach the darkest, most structurally broken moment.

3. FITT 3 SCENE 3 IS THE PIVOT
   "News reaches Beowulf" begins the upward turn.
   Shift to ascending harmonic motion should be clear and marked.

4. TRANSITION MOMENTS ARE CRUCIAL
   - Fitt 2→3: From eruption to helplessness
   - Fitt 3→4: From nadir to hope
   - Fitt 4→5: From passage to threshold

5. LAW IS STRENGTH, NOT WEAKNESS
   Fitt 5's formality is the poem's claim that order is civilization's foundation.
   Music should reflect law as structural beauty.
