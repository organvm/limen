# Current inquiry — the season's live question (the "TBR book" slot)

> A professor's class orbits the book they are writing. This is that slot for the Studium: the single
> question the season's reading, listening, screening, and **trials** are all bent toward — the idea
> being bred and battle-tested toward something publishable. It is **configurable**: replace it each
> season. The synthesis layer (`../synthesis/concepts.yaml` + `counter-systems.md`) is the field of
> candidate inquiries; this file promotes one to the center.

## This season's inquiry

> **Is violence the *truth* of heroic identity, or its dissolution?**

The Iliad makes *mēnis* — wrath — the engine of the whole poem, and never resolves whether Achilles'
rage is righteous or monstrous; it holds both. The Bhagavad Gita, given the same battlefield and the
same paralyzed warrior, answers differently: act, Arjuna, but release the fruit — the self that wrath
would serve is not your real self. One tradition says the hero becomes *most himself* in violence; the
other says rightly-acted violence requires the self to *dissolve*. This is the sharpest of the canon's
counter-systems (`../synthesis/counter-systems.md`: *Achilles ↔ Arjuna*), and it is not resolvable —
which is exactly why it is worth a season.

## How the mediums orbit it
- **Reading** — Iliad 1, 9, 18–22 (the wrath cycle) beside Bhagavad Gita 1–3 (the paralysis and the
  teaching). Read across the counter-system, not in isolation.
- **Music** — the wrath arc (Holst's *Mars*, the Tristan prelude) against the stillness the Gita's
  teaching requires (revelation: Pärt, Tavener). The scores argue the same opposition.
- **Film** — *Apocalypse Now* (wrath unmoored) against a film of renunciation or stilled action; the
  Watch-Along stages the collision on screen.
- **Trial** — the claim *"within the Greek heroic frame, wrath is identity's truth"* defended against
  the Gita's refusal (`trial-protocol.md`, scored by `../rubric/seminar.md`).

## What it produces (the data)
Every orbit emits events (`../analysis/events-schema.yaml`): rubric scores, KEEP/REPLACE verdicts, film
reactions, trial rounds. `../analysis/` packages them into the question's **dossier** — which side keeps
winning trials, which mediums move people most, where the claim had to be narrowed. That dossier is the
draft material the inquiry exists to produce.

## Rotating the inquiry
To change the season's question: pick another counter-system pairing from `../synthesis/counter-systems.md`
(e.g. *fate ↔ wu wei*: the Aeneid's fatum against the Dao De Jing; or *death ↔ impermanence*: the Iliad's
kleos against the Heike's *mono no aware*), rewrite the section above, and re-point the mediums. The
machinery is unchanged; only the question at the center moves. **His gate:** which inquiry is live, and
whether the community is invited into it, are his calls.
