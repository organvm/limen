# SEED — Anthony's directives for the Studium (verbatim)

> Captured 2026-06-22. These govern every decision in the build. Provenance, not paraphrase.

## On reduction — "all avenues"
> "why reduce? always all avenues"
> "reduction naught; all paths explored and visualized — choice is the fruit of life — and thought constructions are cheap"
> answer to every scoping question (delivery / pace / language depth / canon order): **"all"**

**Applied:** all delivery modes (face + document + push), all paces (gentle/standard/intensive), all
language depths (calligraphy / glossed-reading / serious-grammar), and all canon orderings
(interleaved-by-theme / Western-then-Eastern / chronological) are BUILT and surfaced as his living,
switchable choices — never pre-decided down to one. Choice is surfaced, not removed.

## On the shape of the work — consolidate, then expand
> "as you've noticed — a lot of this has been built already in dribs and drabs — imagine a
> consolidation of alchemical reduction albeit evolutionary expansion and massive growth towards
> all avenues explored and all lanes occupied and all pillars constructed"

**Applied:** NOT a from-scratch build. The scattered estate (15-week macro schedule, the 41-work
corpus + LingFrame, Derek `_raw`, carrier-wave list, Wings framework, reading-group patterns) is
**alchemically converged** into one Studium organism via his own converge engine — non-destructively,
losers cited as provenance, gaps surfaced as the growth backlog — then expanded outward until all
avenues/lanes/pillars are filled. "Reduction" here means distillation toward the ideal form, never
janitorial narrowing. ([[distillation-not-reduction]], [[alchemical-convergence-method]])

## On sharing — the community pillar
> "wouldn't this whole process be a useful & valuable thing/set of things to share w a community?
> discord, write posts about it, etc until all pillars erected & avenues explored"

**Applied:** a build-in-public pillar (Discord + posts + public playlists), built and drafted via the
Wings multi-channel pipeline. **Publishing is his gate** — never auto-post/send/invite; reversible
staging only. ([[known-owned-pervasive-then-idgaf]])

## Hard invariants (allwheres)
- **Non-destructive:** absorb / union / copy / draft — never delete, never overwrite originals.
- **Gated irreversibles:** send / publish / invite / live-deploy / wipe are HIS levers, never automatic.
- **No silent "no":** every blocked path cascades to a fallback; the loop never idles.
