Scoring this not as "war" but as its real emotional arc — fate → grief → fate → wrath → wrath. One piece per beat. 👇

1/ Hector alone at the Scaean Gate — the waiting
🎵 Beethoven — Symphony No. 5 — I. Allegro con brio
The four-note fate motif is the correct grammar for this moment. Hector stands outside, immovable — not from courage but from the inexorability of what is coming. Everyone is already inside the walls.
