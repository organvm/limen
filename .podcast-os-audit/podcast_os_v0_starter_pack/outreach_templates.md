# Outreach Templates v0

All external messages must have a real human owner. AI may research, draft, classify, and schedule; it does not impersonate a nonexistent employee.

## Follow-up rule

One routine follow-up after silence. No automated second, third, or fourth nudge. Protected relationships require explicit approval for every message.
