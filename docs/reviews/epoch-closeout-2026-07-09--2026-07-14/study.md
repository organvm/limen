  superseded/unmerged. That is real remote custody, not terminal animation.
- The observed user-led Claude wave produced eight merges (#1037, #1039, #1040, #1042, #1043,
  #1044, #1047, #1045); #1038 and #1041 were explicitly successor-closed. It ended with no dangling

  Main-push CI failed `266` times and succeeded `242` times in the window.
- The work was predominantly custody, classification, lifecycle repair, and control-plane truth.
  Those are enabling credits, but they do not substitute for money, publication, contribution, or
  user-value receipts.
