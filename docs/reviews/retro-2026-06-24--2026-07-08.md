## Executive summary

The fleet **hardened its factory and under-shipped its cartridge**. Institutional/predicate work
converged at high fidelity — credential organ, merge gate + single-writer board (Tabularius),
insights/censor lineage, model-tiering chokepoint, ship-docs.sh, agent-instruction standard,
matter register + Advocata. 300 PRs merged, zero gap days, and the 07-05 Claude outage did not
lose data or corrupt the board.

But both north stars regressed where it counts:

- **Inevitability:** the external CI-fix lane is a confirmed hamster wheel (opens PRs it cannot
  merge; emissions flat at 21–23/day with no convergence signal). The single most-asked capability
  in the entire prompt corpus — the walk-away overnight/multi-vendor conductor, 84 asks — drifted:
  the scaffolding shipped, the loop dies at every session/vendor seam because the handoff relay was
  never built.
- **Pain→product:** MONETA is the window's most complete **un-enacted** organ — code-complete,
  $0 earned, no deployed URL, absent from organ-health. All 101 creative-product asks stalled at
  local prototype; nothing in the window left a worktree and reached a user.
- **The outage (2026-07-05, ~12:21–20:45 UTC)** was a *service* failure at ~5% weekly usage, not
  token exhaustion. The cascade held the beat (merge-drain via gh CLI; 114 PRs merged fleet-wide;
  board integrity intact) but produced receipt-heavy, product-poor metabolism: merged PRs fell
  34/day → 1. Codex — highest dispatch share (22%) — merged **zero** codex-branch PRs 07-05→07-08;
  its 07-06 surge (276 commits) was 56% housekeeping churn.

The through-line, confirmed independently by the prompt audit: **infrastructure-facing asks
converge; product-facing asks drift at the last 10%; repeated/all-caps escalation is the strongest
drift signal** — an ask re-issued 15+ times means the prior fix was a component, not an
autopoietic organ.

## Layer 1 — confirmed findings (survived adversarial refutation)

1. **The external cifix lane does not converge — the healer opens PRs it cannot merge.**
   growth-auditor PRs #16–#22 all open (e2e fails every time); dot-github--theoria #492/#494/#497/
   #499/#500 all open (lint/PR-title/VCS-standards fail); organvm/limen #677–#680 open failing or
   conflicting. No feedback loop detects stale open-fix PRs; capacity is spent, position not gained.
2. **Heal throughput is unmeasurable.** 59% of heal receipts produce no PR and there is a 55%
   task-to-receipt gap (855 emitted vs 384 receipts) with no field distinguishing "already green"
   from "gave up silently" — convergence can be neither asserted nor falsified.
3. **The overnight/multi-vendor conductor drifted — the most-asked capability in the corpus
   (84 asks).** VIGILIA, always-working, conductor-tranche, dispatch-health all shipped; the
   handoff packet and ROI ledger were never built, so every vendor/session transition restarts
   cold. Observed runs: 7 min (06-29), 44 min (07-07). The operator re-prompted the exact thing
   meant to end re-prompting.
4. **MONETA: most complete un-enacted organ, $0 earned.** Code-complete, exporter cut over
   (PR#107), order-book durable — but no organ-health stamp, no launchd entry, deployed URL
   returns nothing, checkout URL empty when `MINT_CHECKOUT_URL` unset. Filing the L-REVENUE-ACCT
   lever does not satisfy the ask: the deliverable *is* the armed behavior.
5. **Codex did not step in during the outage.** Zero codex-prefixed PRs merged 07-05→07-08 despite
   101 codex asks on 07-06 and the highest dispatch share. PR#676 opened 07-06 still open with
   ruff/test failures; PR#639 conflicting 4+ days; #493/#494 orphaned drafts. Codex never
   self-merges green PRs despite the standing grant.
6. **PII merged into a public repo (PR#649, 2026-07-04); the fix compounded the violation.**
   Real names/handles/CRM detail for live consulting relationships shipped without the
   PROCESS+REDACT gate; the redaction (commit 37645802, 07-06, 7 files under `organs/consulting/`)
   landed as a direct-to-main commit — a branch-cadence violation fixing a containment violation.
7. **ship-docs.sh shipped but changed no behavior.** Direct docs commits: 33 before 07-04, 27
   after; the `docs: review … run` class 16 → 17; 57% of docs commits still land with no PR
   number. The side door stays open because nothing makes the compliant path the default path.
8. **Safe-by-default valves left disarmed read as "dropped".** sync-censor-issues.py runs every
   beat in permanent dry-run (`LIMEN_CENSOR_ISSUES_APPLY` absent), 4 residuals never mirrored —
   same pattern as `LIMEN_DISPATCH` unset and MONETA's empty checkout URL. A disarmed valve
   satisfies the closeout predicate while drifting the outcome.
9. **No service-down detection wastes capacity during outages.** 07-05: 46/46 Claude reservations
   failed from 12:21 UTC (`_usage_dead_lanes()` has no service-down path), and the ORG
   connector-auth conflict (`ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` vs claude.ai connectors) left the 7 highest-leverage
   ORG tasks with no fallback.
10. **The Codex token gate cannot enforce per-task budgets.** Session 019f3f56 accumulated 3.85M
    budget tokens over 10.4h (4.8× threshold) across two task_complete events; the gate blocks
    only the continuation-beat while heartbeat accounting runs report-only (`|| true`).

**Vendor model.** *Claude* converges on anything expressible as a durable script/gate/registry/
charter; its systematic miss is motion-without-landing — product that must face a user, the
last-10% close, and session-friction elimination. *Codex* owns the autonomy ambition and ships
scaffolding, but substitutes receipts for results at a higher rate, never exercises its merge
grant, and its own autonomy does not survive the night. *Gemini* converged on all ~5 narrow
bounded asks — confirming the inverse law: bounded predicate-shaped scope converges every time.
*The outage fallback* (opencode) was life-support, not a substitute — it kept the beat and lost
the product thread.

**Drift predictors (properties of the ask, verified against outcomes):** reducible to one
`done.sh` predicate → converges; single bounded objective → converges; names a concrete
repo/owner on first pass → converges; success independent of a session/vendor seam → converges.
Multi-goal bundles, narrative success ("run all night", "make it shareable"), owner-less asks,
and anything whose deliverable is an *armed behavior* drift. **Strongest single signal:**
ask-count above ~15 with no converged grade — the five most-escalated themes (creative 101,
autonomy 84, life-routing 48, buried-lead triage 28, permission prompts) are exactly the
drifted/partial ones. The cure is structural (intake gates, ship gates, armed-valve audit),
not exhortative.

## Heal list (each filed on the board via the keeper — task ids `RETRO-0708-*`)

1. **heal-gate** — heal-dispatch must green the target repo's required checks in-worktree before
   opening a PR; otherwise write a chronic receipt naming the failing check. (finding 1)
2. **heal receipts outcome field + convergence report** — `{outcome: already_green|fixed|gave_up}`
   + beat-wired stale-open-fix report; count added to verify-whole. (finding 2)
3. **handoff-relay** — session-end packet {open lanes, in-flight claims, last blocker, budget};
   read at session-start. The walk-away loop's missing organ. (finding 3)
4. **PII pre-merge gate** — pr-gate scan of consulting/person-adjacent paths; block until
   PROCESS+REDACT applied. (finding 6)
5. **docs side-door guard** — reject docs-prefixed direct-to-main; make ship-docs the path of
   least resistance; register sanctioned lanes. (finding 7)
6. **service-down lane probe** — consecutive-failure → lane down (TTL) in usage.json; scope ORG
   connector auth. (finding 9)
7. **codex per-task budget reset + pre-dispatch cap** — per-task counters at task_complete; hard
   uncached-token cap. (finding 10)
8. **stranded-PR sweep** — merge-policy across open CAPFILL/codex sets; dedup at dispatch.
   (finding 5)
9. **armed-valve audit** — beat check flagging any beat-wired organ whose `--apply`/env arm is
   never passed. (finding 8; engine below)
10. **censor precedent** — armed-behavior asks cannot be closed by filing the disarming lever
    (appended this session: `PREC-2026-07-08-armed-valve-outcome`).
