# Carrier-Wave Media — KERNEL (the cross-platform media domain model)

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is media
> *infrastructure* that AUGMENTS an editor-in-chief / public figure. It does **not** speak for him
> without a gate, alter voice, or invent facts. Every essay, announcement, and social
> post is a drafted proposal the human reviews and owns. The organ scaffolds, distributes,
> and measures; the human publishes.

## Why this organ exists

The ultra-rich have PR teams and media empires that amplify their voice, ensure message discipline across platforms, and maintain consistent publishing cadence. They are not bogged down in formatting newsletters or scheduling tweets. This organ delivers the institutional weight of a cross-platform media empire — the **carrier-wave position** — as a coordinated set of AI roles working under the human's direction. One person, the full apparatus.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to the media domain

| Primitive | Media meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | the audience | the subscriber, follower, or reader reached by the distribution |
| **Mandate** | the message | the essay, product launch, or narrative being broadcast |
| **Standing** | reach / metrics | where the message is now: drafted, scheduled, live, or engaged with |
| **Standard** | editorial voice | the stylistic constraints, tone, and formatting rules — POSSE principles |
| **Governance** | POSSE rules | what gets published, when, and on which channels; never self-acted |

## Fractal deployment

- **MACRO** — a carrier-wave infrastructure any creator can hold: automated essay drafting, templated cross-channel distribution, analytics, and cadence management. Institutional weight for anyone who wants to speak across platforms natively. Generic + nameless underneath; never hardcoded to one person.

- **MICRO** — Anthony's **Carrier-Wave Media** — the pipeline running ORGANVM's public face. Taking the raw system events (sprints, product launches, research) and distributing them via `kerygma-pipeline` to Ghost, Mastodon, Discord, Bluesky, and LinkedIn.

## Hard guardrails (every contributor + every dispatched task)

- **The human's voice is final.** The organ drafts essays and social posts; it never speaks externally without the human's "publish" gate.
- **Do not invent facts.** The organ uses real system metrics and telemetry as source material.
- **No irreversible outward-facing action without the human's gate.** Drafts only. Approval in one context does not extend to the next.
