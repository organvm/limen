## The Instance

The micro deployment is not a demo account. It is Anthony's own financial operating room:

- **Personal household:** personal checking, personal obligations, student-loan risk, card
  interruption, and recurring bills.
- **Commercial vehicle:** Sovereign Systems LLC as the institutional business container, currently
  dormant/blocked for processor KYC until Anthony decides whether to revive, replace, or bypass it.
- **Mission vehicle:** Cind & Sol Foundation as the nonprofit/MPO side, with Quaestor finding grants
  and Aerarium tracking how mission money should be held and governed.
- **Product revenue:** ChatGPT Exporter first, then Public Record Data Scraper, Universal Mail
  Automation, The Invisible Ledger, Mirror Mirror, and Styx as the revenue ladder matures.
- **Sovereign rail:** MONETA at `moneta/` receives processor-free product revenue into a
  principal-held Bitcoin address and mints offline-verifiable product licenses. Aerarium tracks the
  receipt, tax basis, reserve, and disbursement plan around that rail.

The office already knows that intake and institution are separate. MONETA is the rail. Aerarium is
the financial office that records, projects, allocates, and governs what the rail brings in.

## Product Revenue Path

The first-dollar path is the ChatGPT Exporter:

1. Buyer reaches the product checkout.
2. Preferred route: buyer pays through MONETA, which watches the public chain and mints the
   product license after confirmation.
3. Immediate fiat fallbacks: Ko-fi and Lemon Squeezy remain usable individual rails while MONETA
   is deployed and configured.
4. Funds land in a principal-held collection account or wallet.
5. Aerarium records the receipt, assigns tax treatment, updates runway, and stages allocation:
   tax reserve, personal obligations, business expenses, institutional reserve, or mission vehicle.
6. Anthony reviews and executes any actual transfer in the relevant portal.

The dead-LLC/Stripe path is not the default. It is a blocked institutional route to resolve later.
The current route is individual-first because it can receive money now, while preserving a map for
moving institutional revenue into the right vehicle when the entity decision is made.

## Disbursement Logic

Aerarium stages each dollar into a review queue before it leaves the collection point:

| Bucket | Purpose | Current rule |
|---|---|---|
| Tax reserve | Avoid surprise income/self-employment tax exposure | Estimate from receipts; hold until CPA/principal review |
| Personal runway | Keep required personal obligations funded | Prioritize confirmed obligations and debt-risk items |
| Business operating reserve | Domains, cloud services, developer accounts, contractors | Use only when the entity path is clear |
| NPO/mission | Grants, donations, foundation administration | Route only by explicit mission/governance decision |
| Portfolio/wealth | Brokerage, retirement, crypto, emergency reserve | Track as accounts are registered; no trade execution |

The staged output is an instruction packet, not a transaction. The packet answers: source, destination,
amount, reason, tax note, governing entity, due date, and approval gate.

## Wealth And Portfolio Track

The first portfolio version is intentionally conservative:

- Register brokerage, retirement, cash, crypto, loan, and credit accounts in `entities.yaml`.
- Classify each account as asset or liability using the existing account taxonomy.
- Enter balances manually with dates; automation can only deepen after the manual truth is right.
- Keep allocation goals as mandates, not trades.
- Produce a net-position trend from `balances-history.json` as snapshots accumulate.

The office can say "your allocation is drifting" or "your reserve is thin." It cannot buy, sell, rebalance,
or transfer.

## Tax Automation Track

The tax desk should become a continuous workpaper system:

1. Record every product receipt with date, rail, USD equivalent, fees, and entity treatment.
2. Estimate Schedule C/self-employment exposure while the individual rail is active.
3. Mark LLC or nonprofit treatment only after Anthony makes the entity decision.
4. Maintain quarterly estimate windows, filing deadlines, and open document requests.
5. Produce CPA-ready workpapers from the same receipts, obligations, and entity registry.

This is automation of preparation, not automation of filing. A CPA or Anthony validates every tax position.
