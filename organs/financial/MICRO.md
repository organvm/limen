## Disbursement Logic (Where Each Dollar Goes)

Aerarium stages every dollar through a review queue before it leaves the collection
point. The staging is an instruction packet, not a transaction — it answers source,
destination, amount, reason, tax note, governing entity, due date, and approval gate.

| Bucket | Purpose | Rule | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tax reserve | Avoid income/SE tax surprise | Estimate from receipts; hold until CPA review | Principal + CPA |
| Personal runway | Keep obligations funded | Prioritize confirmed + debt-risk items | Principal |
| Business reserve | Domains, cloud, contractors | Only when entity path is clear | Principal |
| NPO/mission | Grants, foundation admin | Only by explicit governance decision | Principal + board |
| Portfolio/wealth | Brokerage, retirement, crypto | Tracked; no trade execution | Principal |

## Tax Automation Track

The tax desk transforms product receipts into CPA-ready workpapers through a
continuous pipeline — no yearly scramble.

### Receipt capture (step 1)

Every product receipt is captured with:
- **Date** of receipt
- **Rail** (MONETA, Ko-fi, Lemon Squeezy, wire, cash)
- **USD equivalent** at time of receipt (from exchange rate at confirmation)
- **Fees** deducted by rail
- **Entity treatment** (individual Schedule C, LLC, or NPO)

### Exposure estimation (step 2)

From receipts, the desk estimates:
- **Schedule C income** — total individual product revenue (all current receipts)
- **Self-employment tax** — 15.3% on net Schedule C income (if individual rail)
- **Quarterly estimated tax** — federal + state installment schedule
- **QBI deduction** — 20% of qualified business income (if applicable)

### Entity routing (step 3 — principal-gated)

Tax treatment changes when revenue moves from individual to entity. The desk
stages both scenarios:

| Entity | Income reporting | SE tax | QBI | Filing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Individual (current) | Schedule C | Yes (15.3%) | Yes (if qualified) | Personal 1040 |
| LLC (if revived) | Schedule C (single-member) | Yes (15.3%) | Yes | Personal 1040 |
| NPO (Cind & Sol) | Exempt (non-profit) | No | No | Form 990 |

Anthony does not need to decide the entity route today. The desk runs both
projections in parallel and preps workpapers for either outcome.

### Filing prep (step 4)

When filing window approaches, the desk produces:
- **Income summary** by entity and rail
- **Deduction workpapers** (business expenses, home office, equipment, software)
- **Estimated vs paid** comparison (installments made vs total due)
- **Document request list** — what the CPA will need that is not yet in the ledger
- **Deadline calendar** — filing dates, extension windows, payment due dates

This is **automation of preparation, not automation of filing.** A CPA or Anthony
validates every tax position before anything is filed.

## What The Beat Does

Every 8 beats (approximately every 16 hours), `scripts/financial-organ.py` runs:

1. Calls `consolidate.py` to regenerate all dashboard artifacts from entity data
2. Assesses maturity against 17 objective criteria
3. Auto-advances the organ-ladder maturity if slices landed
4. Writes `financial-standing.json` to the web face
5. Writes `financial-organ-state.json` for proprioception
6. Stamps the `.voice/financial` timestamp

The organ advances itself. When Anthony enters balances, the maturity auto-bumps.
When real revenue flows, it bumps again. The ladder is lockless and idempotent —
it only moves up, never down, and never requires human attention to advance.

## Operating Constraints (invariant across all entities)

These are not best practices. They are non-negotiable structural constraints:

- **No money movement by the organ.** Every rail ends at Anthony's review-and-execute step.
- **No stored credentials.** Banking/broker APIs are never integrated. Anthony moves money in his own portals.
- **All projections are advisory and assumption-labeled.** No number appears without its caveat.
- **Every tax output is a workpaper for principal/CPA review**, not a filed position.
- **Privacy is structural:** entity boundaries are part of the model, not a UI preference.
- **Unknown is not zero.** Every unknown balance is a visible gap with an owner.
- **MONETA intakes value; Aerarium tracks the institution.** The boundary is structural and precise.
