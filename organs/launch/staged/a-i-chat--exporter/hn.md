## First comment

I built this because once conversations are in the ChatGPT web UI they are hard to move into the systems you already use; the official JSON export is not the same as archiving, citing, searching, and repurposing at the format you need.

What it is: export your ChatGPT history to Markdown, HTML, JSON, PNG, or plain text — browser-only, no account.

Details: live userscript v2.29.1, localized in 9 languages, free with an optional Pro tier (bulk/all-conversation export, conversations.json import/re-export); open source, no backend, no cloud upload.
