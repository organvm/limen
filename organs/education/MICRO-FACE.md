# The Micro Face: Praxis and Instances

The macro thesis of alternative education is backed by grounded, running instances. These instances prove the model. The underlying **Kernel** runs them all seamlessly, whether it's a massive institutional class or a one-on-one mentorship.

Here are the three active pillars of our operational reality:

## 1. ENC1101 (The Institutional Bridge)
Our engagement with the legacy system is highly optimized and automated. The ENC1101 instance at Broward College demonstrates how we collapse bureaucratic overhead to focus purely on high-leverage human intervention.

*   **Zero-Touch Rebuilds:** The recurring labor of term setup is automated. A cartridge-driven generator (`courses/_engine/recapitulate`) digests the master course and generates a dated syllabus, synchronized schedules, intelligent agent XMLs, and precisely dated announcements. What used to take hours takes 30 minutes.
*   **The Feedback Bank:** Instructor energy is preserved for actual pedagogy. Using the universal feedback bank (`universal-feedback-essays.md`), we deploy rich, rigorous, class-wide, and individual critiques based on a finely tuned rubric pattern.
*   **Mastery Over Mechanics:** The system manages the mechanics (conditional release, LTI tools, rubrics) so the instructor can operate at the highest level of mentorship. 

## 2. Derek's Narrative Program (The Private Mentor Model)
This is the canonical **private-classes** skin. It proves our capacity for bespoke, zero-institution, high-leverage mentorship. 

*   **The Apprentice Model:** One learner, fully self-directed but strictly coached. The curriculum is a narrative-development reading arc anchored in McKee's *Story* and McCloud's *Understanding Comics*.
*   **Output-Oriented:** The program is designed to terminate not in a test, but in a real artifact: a messy, complete v1 draft of an original graphic novel.
*   **Rigorous Standards:** Evaluated against our proprietary `derek-narrative-craft-v1` standard (measuring Story Structure, Visual-Verbal Transcendence, Momentum, and Execution-over-Concept). 
*   **Kernel Validation:** This entirely bespoke, 1-on-1 apprenticeship runs on the exact same structural kernel engine that runs the massive ENC1101 shell. No engine edits required.

## 3. The Homeschool Engine (Adaptive Personal Syllabus)
The flagship instantiation of the alt-education supply. This is an AI-personalized education system that transforms generic curricula into context-aware, multi-artifact learning journeys. 

*   **Contextual Rewriting:** Generic advice fails because it assumes the learner will do the translation work. Our engine ingests foundational texts and rewrites every principle, exercise, and recommendation strictly for the learner's specific goals, business, and life context.
*   **The "Wings" Framework:** Education is production, not just consumption. Every completed module automatically generates eight parallel professional artifacts: Academic papers, SOPs, Business cases, Social content, Community prompts, Wiki entries, Web/Blog posts, and Grant proposals. 
*   **Evolving Profile:** The syllabus is a living document. The system maintains a learner profile that evolves based on demonstrated competencies, ensuring progressive complexity from basic logic to kernel engineering to AR/VR integration. Every hour of learning generates reusable professional capital.
