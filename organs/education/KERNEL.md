# Education Organism — KERNEL
## The architecture of an alternative academy

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated throughout this organ):** this is education-infrastructure
> that augments human teachers, mentors, and self-directed learners. It does **not** replace
> pedagogical judgment, enroll students without consent, issue accredited credentials autonomously,
> or submit coursework on behalf of a learner. Every institutional gate — D2L go-live, grade
> submission, curriculum acceptance — stays with the human. The organ runs the institution; the
> person runs the act.

---

## Why this organ exists

The binding constraint on alternative education is not curriculum supply. There is no shortage of
good content. What is scarce is **institutional weight** — the structured memory, repeatable
delivery, credible standards, and observable progression that convert intent-to-learn into
demonstrable outcomes, and convert a parent's or learner's distrust of the legacy system into a
safe, defensible choice.

A well-resourced private academy doesn't wing its syllabi. It doesn't lose track of a learner's
progression between terms. It doesn't hand off a course without a quality gate. Its institutional
floor — rubrics, posture records, structured feedback banks — is what makes it trusted and
replicable.

This organ gives one instructor, one homeschool parent, or one self-directed learner the same
institutional floor — without the overhead of accreditation theater, legacy infrastructure, or
tuition that prices out the people who need it most.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to education

| Primitive | Education meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** (Learner) | the person in the learning relationship | who they are, their context, their goals, their prior preparation, and the constraints that govern their learning act |
| **Mandate** (Quest) | the declared learning goal | what outcome the learner is pursuing, in language that can be checked — not "understand writing" but "produce a structurally sound first draft evaluated against craft criteria" |
| **Standing** (Progression) | where the learner is right now | the explicit stage in the learning arc: intake, active, review, hold, or completed — never "in progress somewhere" |
| **Standard** (Rubric) | what good looks like | per-domain, per-stage criteria that are diagnostic rather than gatekeeping — the bar is named before the learner starts, not revealed at grading |
| **Governance** (Institution) | the authority map | who controls what, what requires the learner's own hand, what cannot be submitted or certified by the system |

The same five primitives map every other organ in the VLTIMA body. Education is not a special
case — it is the same structure with domain-appropriate labels.

## Fractal deployment

### MACRO face — the alt-education platform

The reusable education infrastructure anyone can hold. The quarantine-from-the-system thesis
made operational:

- A **Learner Posture Record** that tracks the learner's mandate, current progression, and
  declared standards from first session to closeout
- A **Quest Architecture** that converts a vague learning goal into a staged, checkpointed arc
  with explicit rubric criteria at each gate
- A **Studium-Generale Provost** — the credentialing organ that norms quality and proves
  outcomes without relying on the legacy institutional chain
- A **Feedback Bank** that preserves instructor energy for high-leverage mentorship by
  systematizing class-wide and individual critique against named standards
- A **Wings Artifact Framework** that turns every completed learning unit into eight parallel
  professional artifacts — the learner exits with evidence, not just grades

The macro form carries no hardcoded learner names, no institution-specific credential paths,
and no idiom tuned to one domain. It is the generic alternative academy. The full platform
description is in [`MACRO-FACE.md`](MACRO-FACE.md).

### MICRO instance — ENC1101 · Derek's narrative workshop · the homeschool engine

Three live deployments that prove the macro platform across different learning contexts:

## The learning posture sequence

```
INTAKE → ACTIVE → REVIEW → HOLD → COMPLETED
```

Posture rules:
- The sequence advances; it does not regress silently
- Progression changes are logged explicitly and require the learner's or instructor's
  confirmation before taking effect
- HOLD is a declared state, not an implied one
- COMPLETED is a full closeout — what was pursued, achieved, deferred, and outstanding

## The authority contract

| What the organ does | What the human does |
|---|---|
| Captures learner context, structures posture record | Confirms the intake is accurate and complete |
| Drafts Quest arc, names rubric criteria | Approves the declared standard before the arc begins |
| Tracks progression, flags blockers and standing changes | Confirms advances and holds |
| Generates feedback bank entries, quality notes | Reviews feedback before delivery to learner |
| Stages credential artifacts, completion records | Submits, signs, and certifies externally |
| Flags institutional gates, surfaces them | Makes the call; the organ never does |

The pattern is: **draft and stage internally; surface to the human for every external act**.
Nothing enrolls itself. Nothing submits itself. Nothing issues itself a credential.

---

## Hard constraints (non-negotiable, every beat)

- **Manual prototype mode.** No autonomous coursework submission, no autonomous grade entry,
  no autonomous enrollment in any external system.
- **No irreversible institutional acts without his hand.** D2L go-live, grade submission,
  public curriculum release: staged, surfaced, held for human action.
- **No accreditation theater.** Every credential this organ issues must be grounded in
  demonstrated, reviewable output — not completion clicks.
- **No invented proof.** Learner progression and outcomes reflect what actually exists in
  the record. Gaps are labeled as gaps, not papered over with confidence.
- **No overreach.** Education infrastructure, not accredited professional certification,
  licensed therapy, or diagnostic assessment.
