# Personal Recovery & Access Protocol

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is health-operations
> infrastructure that augments licensed medical professionals, the patient, and their care team.
> It does **not** replace clinical judgment, diagnosis, treatment, medical advice, or the
> patient-provider relationship. Patient safety, privacy, and the primacy of the licensed care
> chain remain non-negotiable.

## Scope of Matter
**Subject:** Anthony
**Event:** 4th-story fall (~2024)
**Institutional Context:** This protocol operationalizes the "health/recovery + disability-access institution" and directly feeds into the legal organ's ADA accommodation matter.

## 1. Posture & Baseline (Workflow 1: Intake → Posture)
- **Current State:** Post-acute recovery from severe poly-trauma (4th-story fall).
- **Core Limitations:** Sustained mobility constraints, weight-bearing limitations, and stamina gating.
- **Coordination Need:** Continuous tracking of functional capacity to support both physical rehabilitation and legal/ADA workplace accommodations.

## 2. Daily State Record (Workflow 2: State → Record)
- **Capture:** Daily log of pain levels, sleep quality, and mobility capacity.
- **Trend Markers:** Weekly synthesis of functional improvement or regression to inform the care team.
- **Guardrail:** The organ records and charts this data; it *never* interprets clinical significance. All interpretations are deferred to the attending physician.

## 3. Protocol Adherence (Workflow 3: Protocol → Adherence)
- **Regimen:** Active physical therapy exercises, prescribed medication schedules, and clinical follow-ups.
- **Tracking:** Every prescribed action is logged (Done/Skipped/Deferred).
- **Escalation:** Overdue items are flagged for human review (never enforced autonomically).

## 4. ADA & Accommodation Track (Workflow 5: Accommodation → Documentation)
- **Objective:** Maintain an indisputable chain of documentation regarding functional limitations and corresponding accommodation needs.
- **Integration:** Data captured in workflows 1-3 feeds directly into structured accommodation requests.
- **Handoff:** The output of this track is reviewed by the legal organ (and retained legal counsel) to support the ongoing ADA employment matter. No request is sent without direct human approval.

## Execution Requirements
This protocol is an administrative artifact. It requires human validation for all outward-facing actions and relies entirely on licensed clinical directives for its inputs.
