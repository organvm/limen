# Health Organ — KERNEL

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is health-operations
> infrastructure that AUGMENTS licensed medical professionals. It does **not** replace clinical
> judgment, diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice. Patient safety and privacy remain primary.
> Every protocol, care plan, and state record is a support artifact — never a prescription,
> never a diagnosis, never medical guidance independent of a licensed provider.

## Why this organ exists

Recovery from a serious injury — or managing a disability — is a full-time institutional problem that
society asks the individual to solve alone. The wealthy have concierge care teams, patient advocates,
care coordinators, physical therapists, occupational therapists, case managers, and disability-rights
lawyers — a coordinated institutional body. Everyone else is expected to manage appointments, track
symptoms, research protocols, coordinate specialists, document accommodations, and monitor progress
from an exhausted, injured, single mind.

This organ gives one person the institutional weight of a concierge recovery system: structured
protocol tracking, health-state recording, care-plan governance, specialist coordination, and
accommodation documentation — all operating as a reusable health/recovery platform. The same
scaffold serves anyone recovering from injury or managing a disability today and can serve the
next person without being tuned only to one body.

This organ is intentionally **unnamed** at the base. It is not branded to a specific person,
clinic, or condition. The substrate is generic; the instance is personal.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to the health domain

| Primitive | Health meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | **body / patient** | the physical self as the operating subject: capabilities, limits, function, baseline, and recovery goals |
| **Mandate** | **protocol** | the treatment plan that governs action: routines, constraints, therapy cadence, medication schedule, coordination signals |
| **Standing** | **health state** | the current condition baseline: symptoms, trend markers, functional status, progress, change vectors, and flags |
| **Standard** | **clinical guideline** | the verified care logic that frames safe, consistent action: medical best practice, therapy standards, recovery milestones |
| **Governance** | **care plan** | the control structure: specialist coordination, appointment orchestration, scheduling, accommodation obligations, who decides what |

This is the *same* kernel as the legal, governance, social, artist, and consulting organs — only the
domain skin changes. That is the fractal: one structure, every pillar.

## Fractal deployment

### 1) MACRO (generic, nameless foundation)

- A health/recovery + disability-access institution anyone can hold — reusable and legally neutral.
- Protocol indexing: capture any treatment protocol as a structured Mandate with schedule, constraints,
  and milestones.
- Health-state recording: track symptoms, functional status, trend markers, and progress as Standing
  snapshots over time.
- Care-plan orchestration: coordinate specialists, appointments, medication, and therapy as a Governance
  layer — who does what, when, and what triggers escalation.
- Clinical-guideline reference: frame Mandate and Standing against actual medical Standards so protocol
  deviations and progress gaps are visible to the clinician.
- Accommodation-ready record: produce auditable, capability-focused documentation for disability access,
  ADA compliance, leave management, and insurance claims.
- No individual identity is carried in the macro layer. It is a platform, not a patient file.

### 2) MICRO (specific instance on top of MACRO)

- Anthony's post-injury recovery and disability-access case, layered onto the generic substrate.
- Anchor event: 4th-story fall (~2024) — a survivorship injury with long-tail recovery, multi-specialty
  care, and functional-capability rebuilding.
- Operates as an accommodation-ready care-system for the legal ADA matter (the legal organ's live
  employment case): produces auditable, capability-focused records for recovery state, protocol
  adherence, functional limitations, and access needs.
- The micro instance proves the macro platform. Every protocol tracked, state recorded, and care-plan
  coordination that works for this case hardens the reusable layer for the next person.
- **No personal name is used here.** The instance relationship is described; the body is not labeled.

## Hard guardrails (every contributor + every dispatched task)

- **No diagnosis, no prescription, no medical advice.** The organ supports clinical work; it does
  not perform it. Every output is a support artifact for licensed professionals.
- **Do not treat this organ as a doctor, therapist, or hospital.** Patient safety, clinical
  judgment, and privacy outrank every organ operation. If an output could be mistaken for medical
  guidance, flag it.
- **Medical data stays in the licensed care chain.** Records, symptoms, and care-plan artifacts
  are held for the patient and their providers. No sharing, no exposure, no third-party access
  without explicit consent.
- **The organ augments; it does not replace.** Protocol tracking does not replace the therapist.
  Symptom recording does not replace the physician. Accommodation documentation does not replace
  the attorney. Every workflow has a human gate.
- **Disability accommodation records are auditable, not adversarial.** The organ's purpose is to
  produce a faithful, capability-focused record — not to litigate, not to overstate, not to
  understate. The legal organ handles strategy; this organ handles the clinical substrate.
- **No irreversible outward-facing action without gate.** Sharing a health record, filing an
  accommodation request, or communicating a care-plan change to a provider is staged and surfaced
  for human approval. The organ never acts autonomously on protected health information.
