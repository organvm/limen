# Health Organism — KERNEL

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is health-operations *infrastructure*
> that AUGMENTS licensed medical professionals. It does **not** practice medicine, give medical advice,
> or replace doctors, physical therapists, or specialists. Every clinical judgment, diagnosis, and
> treatment decision stays with the licensed provider and the patient.

## Why this organ exists

The healthy have the luxury of taking their bodies for granted. Those navigating severe injury, chronic illness,
or disability are forced to become full-time project managers of their own recovery. The burden of coordinating
specialists, tracking symptoms, managing records, and fighting for access falls on the person least equipped
to carry it. This organ acts as an **institutional prosthesis**: a dedicated care-coordination and recovery-management
system that gives one person the institutional weight of a private health team.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to the health domain

| Primitive | Health meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | body / patient | the physical self, capabilities, baseline, and goals |
| **Mandate** | protocol | the active interventions, daily routines, restrictions, and recovery path |
| **Standing** | health state | where the body is right now: symptoms, metrics, flare-ups, progress |
| **Standard** | clinical guideline | the established medical literature, best practices, and anatomical truths |
| **Governance** | care plan | the coordination of specialists, insurance, appointments, and medical boundaries |

This is the *same* kernel as the legal, education, and financial organs — only the skin changes. That is the
fractal: one structure, every pillar.

## Fractal deployment

- **MACRO** — a health/recovery and disability-access institution anyone can hold: symptom tracking,
  appointment coordination, medical record indexing, and protocol adherence management. Institutional weight
  for any patient navigating the medical system.
- **MICRO** — The active post-injury recovery and disability access management (following a 4th-story fall, ~2024).
  It serves as a single source of truth for the physical state, coordinating physical therapy, specialist visits,
  and daily functional capacity. This ties directly to the legal ADA accommodation matter by providing the
  concrete, documented baseline of capability and requirement.

## Hard guardrails (every contributor + every dispatched task)

- No medical advice. Outputs are trackers, schedules, and organized literature for the patient and their
  licensed doctors to review and act upon.
- The system does not diagnose or prescribe.
- Health data is deeply private: handle it securely. Real medical records and physiological data are the
  patient's to control.
- Ground protocols in real clinical guidelines; do not invent or hallucinate treatments.
