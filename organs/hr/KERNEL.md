## Why this organ exists

Small businesses and solo practitioners usually lack the institutional weight to run a rigorous, compliant, and supportive people-operations practice. Without this, hiring becomes ad-hoc, onboarding is chaotic, compliance is reactionary, and performance management is inconsistent.

This organ's purpose is to act as an **institutional prosthesis** — supplying the backbone as reusable, versioned artifacts. It is the **same 5-primitive kernel** used by every other VLTIMA organ, mapped to the HR domain.

---

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to the HR domain

| Primitive | HR meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | person in a role (employee/candidate/client) | an individual tracked with their history, documentation, and current standing |
| **Mandate** | role or policy | the job description, specific responsibilities, or a policy that governs expectations |
| **Standing** | employment/engagement state | active, on-leave, terminated, interviewing, onboarding, offboarding — the clear status of a member |
| **Standard** | labor-law floor + comp benchmark + policy handbook | the baseline requirements for compliance, compensation fairness, and workplace rules |
| **Governance** | people-ops process + ethics wall | the processes for hiring, firing, review, and the ethical barriers protecting sensitive information |

This is the same 5-primitive map as other organs. Only the domain changes.

## Fractal deployment

### MACRO face — the HR-institution-in-a-box any operator can hold

This face is generic and nameless underneath. It provides a complete institutional foundation that any small business or operator can hold. Its core capabilities include:

- **Hiring loop**: Structured interview rubrics, candidate tracking, and equitable evaluation.
- **Onboarding**: Day 1 to Day 90 ramp-up plans, document collection, and culture assimilation.
- **Handbook**: Living policies that evolve with the organization while maintaining the legal floor.
- **Compliance calendar**: Automated tracking of mandatory trainings, filings, and labor-law updates.
- **Performance framework**: Clear, consistent evaluation criteria and growth pathing.
- **Offboarding**: Secure access revocation, exit interviews, and compliant termination processes.

## Hard guardrails (every contributor + every dispatched task)

- **No automated hiring or firing.** The organ structures the process; the human makes the final decision.
- **Data privacy is absolute.** Personnel records, compensation data, and disciplinary actions are protected behind strict ethics walls.
- **Compliance is a floor, not the ceiling.** The organ enforces legal baselines, but the human sets the cultural standard above it.
- **No irreversible outward-facing action without gate.** Offer letters, termination notices, or policy changes must be reviewed and signed by a human.
