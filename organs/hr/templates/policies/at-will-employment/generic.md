## Policy

Employment with [Company Name] is at-will. This means that either you or the
company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without
cause or advance notice.

Nothing in this policy, any employee handbook, or any company communication
creates an employment contract or alters the at-will nature of employment.

## Notes

- [State] recognizes the at-will employment presumption by statute or common law.
- At-will does not mean employment can be terminated for an unlawful reason
  (discrimination, retaliation, violation of public policy).
- Written disclaimers in the handbook and offer letters protect the at-will
  relationship from implied-contract claims.
