## Required Policies (by headcount)

### All employers
- Equal Employment Opportunity / Non-Discrimination
- Anti-Harassment (including sexual harassment)
- Code of Conduct
- Compensation & Pay Practices
- Work Hours & Attendance
- Expense Reimbursement
- Confidentiality & IP
- Technology & Acceptable Use
- Employee Records & Privacy

### 15+ employees
- Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) policy
- ADA reasonable accommodation process

### 20+ employees
- Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA) compliance
- Older Workers Benefit Protection Act (OWBPA)

### 50+ employees
- Affordable Care Act (ACA) employer mandate notice
- Veterans' reemployment rights (USERRA) policy

## Jurisdiction Notes

- Federal law sets the floor. State and local laws may impose additional requirements.
- At-will employment is presumed unless a contract provides otherwise (not recognized in Montana).
- Handbook acknowledgment forms should be signed and retained in personnel files.
