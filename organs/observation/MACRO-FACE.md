# OBSERVATORY — MACRO FACE (the platform anyone can hold)

The portable form: a **legibility-and-traction engine** any operator or small team can adopt as their
growth floor, independent of ORGANVM's specifics.

## The promise

You have built things. The market rewards *legible* things — one object, one user, one promise, shown
becoming true immediately. MACRO OBSERVATORY watches the field you compete in, benchmarks your own
public surfaces against it *honestly* (controlling for inherited advantage), and hands you **one
reversible experiment at a time** instead of a dashboard of vanity trends.

## What it does, every day

1. **Study winners *and* controls.** For each trending/comparable winner it selects ~3 matched controls
   (same age, category, language, owner archetype, audience, release maturity) that did *not* win — so
   "why they succeeded" becomes a bounded hypothesis, not a survivorship story.
2. **Score mechanisms by transferability.** Every apparent advantage gets an explanatory strength
   (discounted by confounders it can't rule out) and a controllability (can *you* reproduce it now).
   The engine never tells you to "become Apple" — it surfaces the cheap, transferable wins.
3. **Prove your own surfaces true.** It reconciles your public claims (repo counts, capabilities,
   status) against their canonical source, flagging drift and severed pipes before a surface lies.
4. **Propose one experiment.** One reversible change to your highest-leverage surface, with a
   measurement contract (baseline, target, window, failure criterion, reversal path) — and it measures
   whether attention actually converted to use, trust, or revenue.

## Design commitments (why it's trustworthy)

- **Read-only by default.** It proposes; a human approves. It never mutates a public surface or spends.
- **Stars are a signal, not a score.** Success is a vector (reach, activation, retention, trust,
  maintenance, distribution, economic return, cultural impact). Manipulable metrics are flagged, never
  trusted.
- **One entrance first.** It optimizes neither for trend-chasing nor vanity stars — it converts latent
  work into one clear product, then two; scale is *earned through demonstrated use*, not announced
  through counts.

## Adopt it

Point the engine at your repo estate (a ranked value list + an observed inventory), declare which
public surfaces carry which numbers, seed a short competitor list, and arm the daily beat. The floor is
the same loop a platform-engineering org runs on config — pointed at whether the world can understand
and adopt what you make.
