# BIFRONS — Macro face (anyone-deployable)

**A two-way star ↔ contribution portal any maintainer can hold.**

Every repo you star becomes two things at once:

1. **Absorbed material** — a provenance-pinned dossier (identity, license, architecture, contracts),
   scored for resonance against your own repos across three lenses (technical / parallel / kinship),
   and, where it fits, an inbound *transmutation proposal* realized as a **draft internal PR** — never
   a silent code transplant, always license-gated with attribution.
2. **A prepared contribution** — when you hold real evidence (a reproducible defect, a doc ambiguity,
   a missing test), an outbound *contribution packet* is assembled in a disposable, pinned workspace:
   the upstream's own checks, a reproduction, the patch, the PR text — **prepared, sent to no one.**

One `exchange_id` threads the whole traversal, so every artifact is auditable back to the star that
began it. Two scores (how much to absorb vs how much you can give back) stay independent.

**The safety model is the product.** Autonomy defaults to *prepare, never submit*: the loop runs
itself right up to the human gate and stops. Prepared contributions pool and surface as a count. The
only outward act — opening the upstream PR — is one deliberate click; nothing leaves until you make it.

Deploy it as connective tissue over an intake engine, an identity graph, and a compile/decide/execute
engine sharing one small SQLite store — not a new monolith. It runs itself on a heartbeat: absorb new
stars, map resonance, prepare, surface state — asking nothing until the gate.
