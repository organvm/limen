# Sovereign Systems — KERNEL

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is agency-operations *infrastructure*
> that AUGMENTS the delivery of consulting services. It ensures high-quality execution and institutional memory.
> Every client relationship, contract, and final delivery is owned by the human principal (Anthony).
> The organ organizes, scopes, tracks, and drafts so the human professional operates with an agency's leverage.

## Why this organ exists

Great consulting requires more than expertise; it requires institutional process—scoping, delivery management,
quality control, and client communication. The under-resourced solo consultant struggles to scale because
they are doing both the delivery and the administration. This organ gives one person the institutional weight
of a boutique agency, structured as a coordinated set of AI roles working under a real consultant's direction.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to the consulting domain

| Primitive | Consulting meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | the client / stakeholder | the person or org receiving the service; their context and goals |
| **Mandate** | the engagement / SOW | the scope of work, deliverables, and success criteria |
| **Standing** | delivery state | where the project is right now: stage, blockers, timeline, budget |
| **Standard** | quality bar / SLA | the expected standard of work, templates, and methodologies |
| **Governance** | intake->delivery process | who approves what, communication cadence, and feedback loops |

This is the *same* kernel as the legal and education organs — only the skin changes. That is the
fractal: one structure, every pillar.

## Fractal deployment

- **MACRO** — ORGANVM-as-a-service: a consulting-institution platform any independent consultant or small agency can
  deploy: client intake, scoping blueprints, delivery tracking, and automated reporting. Institutional weight for the
  many.
- **MICRO** — Anthony's active client engagements (the Maddie, Rob, and Derek deployments), run with that weight.
  A single source of truth for the engagement state, automated drafting of updates, and quality-checked deliverables
  that Anthony reviews and owns.

## Hard guardrails (every contributor + every dispatched task)

- No final delivery without human review. Outputs are drafts, organization, and tracking
  for the principal to review, correct, and adopt.
- Nothing is sent or signed by the system. The consultant/client decides and acts. Contracts are strictly human.
- Client data is confidential: proprietary material is handled securely; nothing leaves the designated channels.
