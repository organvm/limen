# Legal Organism - KERNEL (Cochran / FLAGSHIP)

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is legal-operations
> infrastructure that augments licensed counsel. It does **not** give legal advice, practice law,
> file pleadings, send communications, negotiate, select strategy, or create an attorney-client
> relationship. Every legal judgment, privilege decision, filing, settlement decision, and court
> strategy remains with the attorney of record and the client.

## Purpose

The legal organ is an **institutional prosthesis** for representation: the missing firm-weight that
lets one person face a legal system with organized facts, owned deadlines, indexed evidence,
controlling standards, and reviewable work product instead of an exhausted private mind.

It is not a tip jar and not a chatbot. It is the operating kernel for a legal institution:
case-management discipline, evidence-room discipline, research-room discipline, deadline discipline,
and ethics-wall discipline, all held in a form a licensed attorney can supervise and adopt.

Anthony named the flagship legal instance **Cochran**. That name belongs to the top layer: the
flagship deployment. Underneath it, the kernel stays generic and nameless so the same legal
institution can be held by another person, clinic, pro-se litigant, attorney, or under-resourced
community without carrying Anthony's facts as substrate.

## Layering Rule

1. **Domain-neutral kernel** - the universal primitive map: Member, Mandate, Standing, Standard,
   Governance.
2. **Legal organ** - the reusable legal-institution platform built from those primitives.
3. **Cochran flagship instance** - Anthony's active ADA employment matter, run with top-firm
   operating weight while **augmenting attorney Micah Longo**.

The lower layer must remain generic. Anthony's matter, Micah's name, ADA posture, and any live facts
belong only in the micro deployment and attorney-facing work product. This keeps the organ portable
while still letting the flagship prove itself on the real matter.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to law

| Primitive | Legal domain mapping | What the organ tracks |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | **client / party** | The represented person or entity, role in the matter, identity protections, authority to act, desired relief, and consent boundaries. |
| **Mandate** | **claim / matter** | The legal problem being advanced: claims, causes of action, defenses, requested relief, theory of the case, scope of work, and open questions for counsel. |
| **Standing** | **case posture** | The current procedural and factual posture: stage, deadlines, risks, leverage, obligations, available moves, evidentiary gaps, and what requires attorney judgment. |
| **Standard** | **statute / precedent / rubric** | The governing authorities and proof requirements: statutes, regulations, controlling precedent, local rules, administrative rules, evidentiary requirements, and attorney-approved rubrics. |
| **Governance** | **the firm's process + ethics wall** | Who may decide what, what must be reviewed, what cannot self-act, conflict checks, confidentiality, privilege handling, chain of custody, retention, and UPL boundaries. |

This is the same five-part kernel used by the other organs. The legal skin changes the meaning of
the primitives; it does not change the primitive structure.

## MACRO deployment: legal institution anyone can hold

The macro form is a legal-institution platform any pro-se or under-resourced person can hold. It is
portable, reusable, and deliberately nameless at the foundation.

Its first operating surfaces are:

- **Matter intake** - Member and Mandate captured without pretending the system is counsel.
- **Case-posture brief** - Standing rendered as a living procedural/factual posture record.
- **Evidence index** - documents, messages, records, dates, sources, and chain-of-custody notes.
- **Standards map** - statutes, precedent, rules, and rubrics linked to elements and proof needs.
- **Deadline and obligation ledger** - dates, owners, lead times, and review gates.
- **Attorney-review workflow** - drafts and analyses routed to licensed counsel for correction,
  adoption, rejection, or silence.
- **Ethics wall** - privilege, confidentiality, conflicts, UPL, retention, and authority controls
  enforced before convenience.

In macro form, the organ does not know Anthony. It knows only how a legal institution holds a matter
without crossing into the practice of law.

## MICRO deployment: Anthony's ADA employment matter

The micro form is Anthony's active ADA employment matter, run with the operating weight of a
top-tier firm while **augmenting attorney Micah Longo**.

The target is not "AI lawyer." The target is "Micah has the institutional bench a top firm would
put behind the matter":

- a current case-posture brief,
- a clean evidence and chain-of-custody index,
- an elements-to-evidence map for attorney validation,
- a deadline and obligation ledger,
- reviewable draft skeletons,
- explicit open questions for counsel,
- and a record of what the system must never do without human authority.

Micah remains counsel. The system does not replace him, advise around him, file around him, or
communicate around him. It prepares organized work for attorney review so the live matter can carry
institutional weight without pretending a machine has a law license.

## Flagship constraints

- **The flagship is Cochran.** The named top layer is Anthony's first legal deployment; the base
  kernel remains generic and nameless.
- **Augment real counsel.** Every artifact is built to strengthen licensed counsel's review,
  strategy, and execution, especially Micah Longo's work on the ADA matter.
- **Never practice law.** No advice, no representation, no filings, no service, no negotiation, no
  autonomous legal communication, no court appearance, no legal conclusion presented as final.
- **No invented authority.** Statutes, cases, rules, deadlines, and citations are real or absent.
  Unverified authority is marked as a research question, not as law.
- **No private facts in the substrate.** Live matter facts stay in the micro layer and in
  attorney/client-controlled work product, not in the generic platform.

## Governance gates

The Governance primitive is the organ's ethics wall. These gates are mandatory:

| Gate | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Counsel review | Legal analysis, strategic recommendations, filings, correspondence, and demand language require attorney review and ownership. |
| Client authority | Settlement positions, factual representations, disclosures, and waivers require explicit client authority. |
| Privilege handling | Privileged material stays scoped to the attorney-client channel and is never exposed for convenience. |
| Chain of custody | Evidence is indexed with source, date, provenance, and handling notes; missing provenance is marked as a gap. |
| Citation integrity | Legal authority must be source-backed and jurisdiction-aware; uncertain points become questions for counsel. |
| Outward action | Filing, service, sending, publishing, negotiating, or committing the client is staged only, never autonomous. |
