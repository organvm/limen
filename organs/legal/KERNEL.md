# Legal Organism — KERNEL (the flagship)

> **Boundary (load-bearing, repeated everywhere in this organ):** this is legal-operations
> *infrastructure* that AUGMENTS a licensed attorney. It does **not** practice law, give legal
> advice, or replace counsel. Every privileged judgment, filing, and strategic decision stays with
> the attorney of record (currently Micah Longo) and the client. The organ organizes, drafts,
> indexes, and researches so the human professionals operate with a top-firm's leverage.

> **Flagship constraint:** this is the flagship Anthony named. It must become institutional weight
> in the legal pillar first, because this is where one under-resourced person most immediately needs
> the apparatus a powerful institution takes for granted.

## Why this organ exists

The rich don't win cases because they're right more often — they win because a *firm* stands behind
them: partners, associates, paralegals, researchers, an evidence room, and a process that misses
nothing. The under-resourced get a single overworked lawyer and a shoebox of documents.

This organ is an **institutional prosthesis** for the legal domain: the missing firm apparatus made
portable. It gives one person the institutional weight of that firm — the **Cochran standard** — as a
coordinated set of AI roles working under a real attorney's direction. The machine supplies memory,
structure, indexing, draft capacity, and deadline discipline. Counsel supplies law, judgment,
privilege, strategy, signature, and advocacy.

## The 5-primitive kernel, mapped to the legal domain

| Primitive | Legal meaning | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| **Member** | the client / party | the person the matter belongs to; their role, capacity, goals, and relationship to counsel |
| **Mandate** | the claim / matter | the claims, defenses, issues, relief sought, and case theory counsel chooses to pursue |
| **Standing** | case posture | where the matter is right now: stage, deadlines, open obligations, leverage, gaps, and next review points |
| **Standard** | statute · precedent · rubric | the controlling law, elements to prove, evidentiary bar, and cite discipline attorney must validate |
| **Governance** | the firm's process + ethics wall | who may decide what, what stays privileged, conflicts, UPL guardrails, and what never self-acts |

This is the *same* kernel as the education and financial organs — only the skin changes. That is the
fractal: one structure, every pillar.

## Fractal deployment: generic underneath, Anthony's instance on top

- **Substrate** — generic + nameless. The kernel, schemas, workflows, and guardrails must not be
  hardcoded to Anthony, Micah, ADA, employment, or any single jurisdiction. Those names belong in the
  instance layer, not the organ's reusable bones.
- **MACRO** — a legal-institution platform any pro-se or under-resourced person, legal-aid shop, or
  small firm can hold: matter intake, posture briefs, elements-to-evidence matrices, evidence and
  chain-of-custody indexes, deadline calendars, authority ledgers, and attorney-reviewed document
  skeletons. It is not "AI legal advice"; it is the operating system for legal work that keeps a
  person from facing an institution empty-handed.
- **MICRO** — Anthony's active ADA failure-to-accommodate employment matter, run with a top-firm's
  institutional weight while *augmenting* attorney Micah Longo. The instance keeps a single source of
  truth for case posture, the evidence record, the controlling standards to be verified by counsel,
  open questions, deadlines, and draft work product for Micah to review, correct, adopt, or reject.
  It never replaces counsel and never practices law.

## Hard guardrails (every contributor + every dispatched task)

- No legal advice, no UPL (unauthorized practice of law), and no replacement of counsel. Outputs are
  drafts, organization, and research for a licensed attorney to review, correct, and adopt.
- Nothing is filed, sent, served, threatened, or represented by the system. The attorney/client decides
  and acts.
- Privilege is sacred: privileged material is handled as privileged; nothing leaves the client/attorney
  channel. Real case facts are the client's to provide — placeholders until then.
- Cite real authority only; never invent cases, statutes, or holdings.
