> Boundary: this packet is legal-operations work product for review by

This is the first working vertical slice of the legal organ for one live
matter: Anthony's ADA employment/accommodation matter, explicitly augmenting
attorney Micah Longo. The slice proves the organ can turn the abstract
`KERNEL.md` and `CHARTER.md` into a concrete matter packet:

- `intake.md` - what is known, what is deliberately unknown, and who must
  confirm it.
- `posture.md` - the current case-posture brief.
- `evidence-index.csv` - the evidence and chain-of-custody index.
- `chain-of-custody.md` - custody handling rules and the current chain table.
- `deadlines.md` - attorney-confirmed and unconfirmed date posture.
- `ethics-log.md` - UPL, privilege, conflict, and review gate certification.
- `MICAH-FRAMEWORK-DECK.md` - the presentation wrapper for Micah.
- `drafts/MICAH-COVER-NOTE-DRAFT.md` - optional outbound note, staged only.

## Current standing

`ATTORNEY_REVIEW_PACKET_STAGED`

The matter packet is real in the sense that it is bound to the named live
matter, named counsel, the repository's legal/health organ record, and the
current first artifact set. It is also honest about what has not been supplied:
private employment records, accommodation correspondence, deadlines, venue,
case number, and counsel-approved claim theory are not present in this public
repo. They must be supplied through a privileged channel before the evidence
index can become litigation-grade.
