# Chain-of-custody index - Anthony ADA employment matter

> Boundary: custody tracking only. This does not decide admissibility, relevance,
> privilege, or legal strategy. Counsel verifies every item before use.

## Custody rule

Every source item must have:

- a stable `evidence_id`;
- date or date range;
- source kind and source location;
- custodian or source;
- provenance statement;
- custody path;
- privilege/confidentiality posture;
- attorney review status.

Private employment, health, and privileged records must not be pasted into this
public repository. This packet indexes their needed categories and waits for a
privileged-channel intake path approved by Micah.

## Intake procedure for future private records

1. Receive record only through a counsel/client-approved channel.
2. Assign a stable ID before summarizing or drafting from it.
3. Record the original custodian, date received, storage location, and any
   redaction applied.
4. Mark privilege/confidentiality before the record is referenced elsewhere.
5. Require Micah's review before any element map, chronology, or draft uses it.
