# Closeout contract

- Keep this capsule single-purpose; route another lane through its own owner and capsule.
- Keep generated, private, and heavy files classified.
- Push useful source work or create a durable owner receipt before local cleanup.
- Never delete personal/private data without its explicit custody gate.
- Preserve the existing 7.456 GiB Archive4T output in place; do not move or delete it without the
  two-copy/restore gate.
- Leave no unowned Scratch output. A failed first full attempt needs its exact stage repair receipt;
  a failed second attempt is terminal for that frozen snapshot/config.
- Run each implicated focused predicate once per unchanged head, record it, and reuse that receipt.
  Do not repeat local suites to accumulate reassurance; only a changed head or a specific observed
  failure authorizes another test.
- Record changed paths, the exact predicate/result, local and remote custody, verified final hashes,
  and any named owner-routed residual.
- At a session boundary, leave the next capsule index and one launch command.
